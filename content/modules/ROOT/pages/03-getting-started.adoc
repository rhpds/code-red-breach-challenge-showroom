== Getting started

1. The mission tracking and flag submissions happen on the **CTF Dashboard** (first tab). 

* That's the first thing you need to open and log into using your credentials.

2. Open and log in the **OpenShift Web Console** (second tab)

* You'll land in the namespace of the application that has been hacked — make sure to switch to your namespace otherwise.
* You'll notice that a pipeline has run, which created the malicious application, but the hacker somehow managed to destroy the image before leaving — which explains why the Pod is missing.
* First, pay attention to the PipelineRun object, then take it from there.

Good luck!
